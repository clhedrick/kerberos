You can gotten an "access denied" error. Unless you're poking around where you shouldn't be, likely causes include
  • Your login session has expired.
  • There's something wrong with cookie handling in your browser.
We suggest going back to the main page and going through login again. If that fails, try restarting your browser. If you get this problem frequently, please report it to help@cs.rutgers.edu with as many details as possible about what you were doing.
